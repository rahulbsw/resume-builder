## Missing Information To Ask User

Ask concise questions for gaps that block a strong interview story or accurate timeline.

| Gap | Why It Matters | Suggested Question | Priority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Missing dates | Timeline accuracy | What were the start and end dates for this role or project? | High |
| Missing metric | Impact strength | What changed after this work: latency, cost, adoption, incidents, uptime, productivity, revenue, or customer outcome? | High |
| Unclear ownership | Interview credibility | What part did you personally design, build, review, lead, or operate? | High |
| Missing scale | Technical depth | How many users, services, teams, regions, requests, data volume, repositories, or environments were involved? | Medium |
| Sensitive detail | Disclosure safety | Should this name, metric, repository, customer, or project be anonymized? | High |
